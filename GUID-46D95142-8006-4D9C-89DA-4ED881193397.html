Entering Income Statements for a Small Business Application

The Income Statements sub-pane appears under Business Financial Details in the Create Application page for a small business applications.
To enter the income statements for a small business application, perform the following steps:
  1. Enter Income statements details in the fields provided, including those under Other Income and Operating Expenses.
  2. (Optional) Use the additional tabs on the Income statements sub-pane to enter the income details for the previous two years.
