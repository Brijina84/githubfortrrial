My Queues

The My Queues pane on the Home page provides a way to view and access queues you are assigned to. Queues are groupings of applications selected based on a defined criteria; they control the sequence in which applications become available and the persons who can access them. Users and groups can be assigned to queues using the Queues management page.

If queues have not been enabled for your groups or roles, no queues appear in the My Queues pane.
