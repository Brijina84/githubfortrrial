Application Processing Module Reports

The Application Processing Module provides a variety of reports to assist you in evaluating the effectiveness of your system. If the role to manage reports has been assigned, you will have access to view the reports.
Reports are grouped into two categories:
  • Operational Reports
    • Application Status Summary
    • Queue Summary
    • Operator Performance
    Note These reports are available for both Consumer and Small Business credit applications.
  • Score Reports
    • Final Score
    • Approval Rate Decomposition
    Note These reports are available for the Consumer credit applications.
Covered Topics
  • Operational Reports
    Operational reports describe the state of the system during the report period specified.
  • Score Reports
    Score reports distill a large amount of data into simplified metrics that help you evaluate how well your score models are working.
