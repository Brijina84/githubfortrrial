Sub-process - Customer Eligibility Check

The Customer Eligibility Check sub-process is a pre-configured out-of-the-box sub-process available for use in a business process. The sub-process checks the eligibilty of the applicant against four parameters, gross monthly income, net monthly income, age, and postal code. The sub-process makes a call to the pre-configured decision service to perform the eligibility check. The decision service returns two outputs, decision and status. The output decision can have a value such as, review, decline, or investigate. The output status indicates whether the call was successful or whether an error occurred.
Note When the processing workflow calls the decision service for a decision, the decision service returns the decisions and reasons to the client application and the information is saved in the application BOM.

The sub-process picks the values of the four parameters from the credit application and makes a call to the decision service. Based on your business needs, you must configure business rules using the business strategies workbench. Origination Manager generates a decision service from these rules. The sub-process invokes this decision service which returns a decision based on the rules configured.
