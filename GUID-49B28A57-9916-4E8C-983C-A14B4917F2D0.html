Deleting a Product Offering

Use the Remove current offer icon in the Manage Product Offerings section to remove a product offering specified for a pricing scenario.
  1. In the Scenarios list page, select the pricing scenario you want to modify.
  2. If the pricing scenario is not checked out, click Check Out.
  3. If the pricing scenario is checked out, click Edit.
  4. Click the Remove current offer icon for the product offering you want to delete.
  5. When prompted, click Yes to confirm the deletion.
  6. Click Save.
