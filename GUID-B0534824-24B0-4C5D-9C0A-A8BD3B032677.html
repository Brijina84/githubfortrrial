Working With Products

A product can be a loan, credit card, or other service products provided by an institution. Products are associated with a product category. For each product, you may define multiple champion-challenger strategies and associated decision flows that execute rulesets, decision tables, decision trees, score models, and data method sequences.
Note If your system includes both the Decision Module and the Application Processing Module, the Application Processing Module makes a call to the Decision Module by submitting XML request that specifies the category and product to use for processing an application. For more information about the use of categories and products with the preconfigured solution, see the FICO Origination Manager Decision Module Developer's Guide.
Covered Topics
  • Product Properties
    Product properties are defined in the Product Properties page. Select a product and navigate to the Product Properties page when you add or modify products.
  • Creating a Product
    A product is always associated with a category. In the Category Properties page, use the Add icon in the Products section to create products.
  • Renaming a Product
    Rename a product from the Product Properties page.
  • Deleting a Product
    Use the Delete icon in the Products section to delete a product that is not required.
  • Product Check Out, Cancel Check out and Check In
    The SMW enables multiple users to work on products by allowing you to check out, check in and cancel checkout of products.
