Products

Use the Application Processing Module to manage product details for all offered product categories (loan types). Administrators with the proper permissions can create, modify, view, activate, deactivate, or delete products from the system.

Products data includes model category, active dates, fees, rates, and more. The combination of Product Category Type and Product determines the rules used in the Decision Module.

Note Product names must be unique within a product category.
Covered Topics
  • Adding Products
    Use the Products > Create Product page to add products to the system.
  • Modifying Products
    Use the Products > Edit <Product Name> page to modify products and product details in the system.
  • Deleting Products
    Administrators with the proper permissions can delete products from the system. You cannot delete a product that is associated with an application.
  • Activating and Deactivating Products
    Each product in the Application Processing Module has a status of either Active or Inactive.
