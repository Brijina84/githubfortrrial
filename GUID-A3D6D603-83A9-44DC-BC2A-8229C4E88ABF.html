Deleting a Client

Use the Clients page to delete a client.When you delete a client, the client users can no longer access the system. You must have the ManageClients role to delete a client.
Note You cannot delete a client in progress.
  1. Log on to the APM user interface.
  2. In the administration navigation pane, click Security > Clients.
  3. (Optional) In the search area field, enter the search filter criteria and click Search.
  4. Click the Delete icon in the row of the client that you want to delete.
  5. Click OK in the confirmation box.
