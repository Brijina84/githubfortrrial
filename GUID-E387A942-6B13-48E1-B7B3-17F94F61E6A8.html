Pricing Scenarios

A pricing scenario is a mechanism for recording product offers from within an action node of a decision tree. Each pricing scenario specifies parameters for one or more product offers, including offer ID, interest rate, credit limit, and term. When a pricing scenario is executed, it creates, under the DecisionResponseâ€™s Product object associated with the current product, one Offer object per offer, populated from the information provided when the pricing scenario was defined. If desired, other Offer object fields may be populated by executing a data method sequence in the same decision tree action node, or in a later decision flow step.
Covered Topics
  • Pricing Scenarios Properties
    The Pricing Scenario Properties page displays the properties associated with a pricing scenario, such as the interest rate and the amount of a product offering.
  • Creating a Pricing Scenario
    Use the Create button on the Scenarios list page to add a new pricing scenario.
  • Copying a Pricing Scenario
    Use the Save As icon in the Pricing Scenario Properties page to add a new pricing scenario based on an existing one.
  • Deleting a Pricing Scenario
    Use the Delete icon on the Scenarios list page to delete a pricing scenario that is no longer needed.
  • Deleting a Product Offering
    Use the Remove current offer icon in the Manage Product Offerings section to remove a product offering specified for a pricing scenario.
