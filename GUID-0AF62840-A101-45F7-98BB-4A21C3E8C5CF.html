Decision Module Overview

The FICO® Origination Manager Decision Module is an account origination product that leverages the full power of the FICO® Blaze Advisor® business rules management system. It provides companies with proven rules-management technology that supports the entire process for designing, building, and maintaining rules-based decision applications.

The Decision Module is designed for new account origination. It is ideal for building and maintaining the policies, practices, and procedures that define the complex business strategies found in the origination process.

The Origination Manager Decision Module enables the development, testing, and deployment of business rules. The Decision Module includes a Strategy Management Workstation (SMW) that allows you to develop originations strategies by creating and maintaining lending product strategies through graphical and intuitive rule structures. The Strategy Management Workstation provides following item types to develop business strategies:
  • Decision Flows
  • Rulesets
  • Score Models
  • Scenarios
  • Decision Trees
  • Decision Tables

It also includes the Decision Module web service to enable runtime execution of your strategies, the Data Storage Service (DSS; also a web service) to store your decision data in a database, and the Strategy Testing Facility (STF), a tool used to extract testing data from the data repository for testing purposes.

The Decision Module provides tools that facilitate champion-challenger and end-to-end strategy testing. It facilitates custom model deployment and characteristic generation.
