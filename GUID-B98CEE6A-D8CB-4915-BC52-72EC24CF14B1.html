Canceling a Product Check Out

Use the Cancel Check Out icon in the Product Properties page to undo a product check out.
  1. In the categories list page, click the category containing the product.
  2. In the product list, click the product.
    The Product Properties page for the product appears.
  3. Click the Cancel Check Out icon.
  4. When prompted, select Yes to cancel checkout of a product.
    For more information on canceling a check out, see Canceling a Checkout.
