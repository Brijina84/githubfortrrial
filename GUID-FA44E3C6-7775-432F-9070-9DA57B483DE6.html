Modifying Product Categories

Use the Categories > Edit <Category Name> page to modify products and product details in the system.
  1. In the administration navigation pane, click Categories.
  2. (Optional) In the search area field, enter search filter criteria and click Search.
  3. Select the product category to modify and click the Edit Category icon for that row.
  4. Make the desired modifications to the product category information. For more information about how to enter information in product category fields, see Adding Product Categories.
  5. Click Save.
