Modifying the List of Fields Tracked for Data Change

Use the Data Change Actions page to modify the list of fields that are tracked for any data change and the actions to be taken in case of a data change.
  1. In the administration navigation pane, click Data Change Actions.
  2. In the Require Restart area, add a field by selecting it from the Available list and clicking Add. To remove a field from the Selected list, select the field and click Remove.
  3. In the Require Rescore area, add a field by selecting it from the Available list and clicking Add. To remove a field from the Selected list, select the field and click Remove.
  4. Click Save to save your changes.
