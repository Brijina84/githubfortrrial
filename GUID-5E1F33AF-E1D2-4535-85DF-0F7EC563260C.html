CRA Score Reason Code Values

When online credit bureau scoring models are executed, they produce reason codes that represent the reasons that impacted the online CB score. These reason codes are also referred to as "online CB score factors".
See Also For information on score factor substitution, see the Analytic Module chapter in the FICO Origination Manager Integration Guide.
