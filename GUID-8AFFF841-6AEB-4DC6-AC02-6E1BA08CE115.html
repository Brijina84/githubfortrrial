Opening Reports

If reports were made available to you, open them from the My Reports pane on the Application Processing Module Home page. You can also access any of the available reports using the Reports option in the task menu.
To open reports, perform one of the following steps:
  • Under the My Reports pane, click the report you want to open.
  • Click the Reports option in the task menu and then click the report you want to open.
The report opens with options at the top of the page, such as the from and to date, to filter and refine your focus.
Covered Topics
  • Filtering Data in Reports
    When you initially generate a report, and it contains more data than you need, you can narrow the data shown in the report by using filters that query the database.
  • Exporting Reports to Excel
    If you want to save, further manipulate, or print report information, export the report to Microsoft Excel.
