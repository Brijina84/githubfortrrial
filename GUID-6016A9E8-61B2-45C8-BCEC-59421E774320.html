Deleting a Product

Use the Delete icon in the Products section to delete a product that is not required.
  1. In the categories list page, select the category containing the product you want to delete.
    The Category Properties page appears.
  2. Click Check Out if the item is not checked out.
    To delete a product, you must check out the category.
  3. In the product list, select the product and click the Check Out icon.
    You must check out the product before you can delete the product.
  4. Click the Delete icon.
    Note The Decision Module prevents you from deleting an item that is referenced by another item. Use the Where Used search feature to find the references. Before you delete the item, remove all references to it.
  5. When prompted, select Yes to delete the product.
    The product name disappears from the product list.
    Note Deleting a product in a category dissociates the product from the category permanently. It is possible to undo the deletion from your private workspace by clicking Cancel Check Out for the category.
  6. Click Save.
