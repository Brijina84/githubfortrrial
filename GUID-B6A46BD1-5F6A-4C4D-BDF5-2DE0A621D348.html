Product Strategies

Product Strategies are specialized decision trees that determine which decision flow executes for a product. Access Product Strategies from the Item Types tab.

A product strategy’s decision tree indicates the conditions under which the product executes a decision flow associated with a action. A product strategy's decision tree has condition and action nodes. Condition nodes control branching within the tree. Action nodes specify that a single decision flow is run or indicate that no decision flow is run .

A product strategy can contain just one decision flow or it can contain multiple flows. The Decision Module evaluates the conditions and chooses to run one decision flow. A product strategy is assigned to a product in the Product Properties page.

If a product does not have an assigned product strategy or if the product strategy does not specify a decision flow, the product’s default decision flow is used if one has been specified. If you attempt to process applications using a product that does not have a product strategy or a default decision flow, the Decision Module returns an error message.
