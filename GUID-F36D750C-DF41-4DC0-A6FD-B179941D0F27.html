Getting Started/Typical Workflow/Client Onboarding

To quickly start with the processing of your applications, configure your Origination Manager setup. Origination Manager comes with a pre-configured setup, complete with a data model, application processing controls, business rules and strategies, and a processing workflow. Refer to the following tasks, perform the tasks, and change the default configuration where required.

1. Select your Line of Business

Select the line of business that you operate in. Each Line of business template comes with its pre-configured data model that captures most of the data requirements and the data relationships for that line of business.

2. Configure the data sources

Set up the access to your data sources,

3. Configure your categories and products

Configure the different categories of products and then create the various products that you want to offer to your customers in a specific category.

4. Add users and assign roles

Create accounts for your users and assign them appropriate roles with the necessary privileges. Also, create a second system administrator account with full permissions. You might need this account if you are unable to log in with the default system admin account and thereby, prevent any disruption.

For more information, see Creating Users

5. Configure your Data Model

Based on you business domain, easily adapt the data model to suit your needs. Add, edit, or delete objects, fields, attributes, and relationships.
