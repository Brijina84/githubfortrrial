Viewing Application Overview Data for a Consumer Credit Application or a Synchronous Consumer Credit Application

The Application Overview pane on the Application page for a consumer credit application or a synchronous consumer credit application provides the following summary information about the applicant and, if it is a joint application, for the co-applicant:
  • Name
  • Present address
  • Identification number
  • Phone number
  • Credit Score (If the Credit Score is less than the population score cut-off value, the value in the score bar appears red. If the Credit Score is equal to or greater than the population score cut-off value, the score bar appears green.)
  • Depending on the status of the application, a bar graph showing the applicant's current monthly debt-to-income ratio, total monthly housing expenses, and the total balance of both and/or a bar graph showing the applicant's current total assets, total monthly income, and the total monthly liabilities.
Note If you choose to mask certain personal identifiable information (PII) on the UI, you must use the field masking or component authorization techniques available for these pages. For more information, contact FICO Product Support.
