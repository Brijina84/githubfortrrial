Modifying Products

Use the Products > Edit <Product Name> page to modify products and product details in the system. Modifications made to a product are not applied to applications that are in progress for that product.
  1. In the administration navigation pane, click Products.
  2. (Optional) In the search area field, enter search filter criteria and click Search.
  3. Select the product to modify and click the Edit Product icon for that row.
  4. Make the desired modifications to the product information. For more information about how to enter information in product fields, see Adding Products.
  5. Click Save.
