Avoiding Name Conflicts

With the Decision Module, when naming objects, fields, or SMW components (such as rules, decision tables, and decision trees), use combinations of alphanumeric characters (A-Z, a-z, 0-9) and the underscore (_) only; do not use spaces. You cannot use reserved words, because these names are already taken. Blaze Advisor reserved words are listed in the following table. Decision Module reserved words are listed in the Decision Module Domain Model Constraints chapter of the FICO Origination Manager Decision Module Developer's Guide.
