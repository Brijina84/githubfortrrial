Copying a Product Strategy

Use the Save As icon in the Product Strategy Properties page to create a product strategy from an existing product strategy.
  1. In the Product Strategy Properties page, click the Save As icon.
  2. In the Name field, enter a name for the new product strategy.

    Names must be text strings of 50 characters or less and can contain letter, numbers, and underscores.

  3. Click Save As.

    The Product Strategy Properties page for the new product strategy appears.

  4. Click Edit, and modify the description and any other properties you want to change.
  5. Click Save.
