Integration with Decision Modeler

The Business Strategies workbench is integrated with FICO® Decision Modeler. You use the workbench to author business rules which are used for decisioning your credit applications. The Decision Modeler configures a decision service from the rules that you have authored and deploys it in the FICO® Decision Management Platform (DMP). A decision service automates a decision-making task. It is implemented as a set of business rules and exposed as a web service.

Business processes or workflows are configured to process enterprise credit applications. When the credit application requires a decision based on the business rules and strategies, the workflow calls a decision service. For example, you can evaluate a loan application by developing a decision service that takes the loan application data as input and returns the loan application status as the output. You configure the decision service and deploy it in DMP. This decision service is then accessed by a workflow that processes credit application.

A decision service includes an entry point that defines the data to be processed, the business rules that evaluate the data, and a decision flow that defines the order in which the data is evaluated by the decision entities. When the decision service for business strategies is deployed, REST endpoints are generated. A swagger.json is provided to describe the REST endpoint schema.
Note You can copy and paste the swagger.json contents into a browser to see the REST endpoint schema or you can copy and paste it to a Swagger online editor to see the JSON file in more a readable format.
