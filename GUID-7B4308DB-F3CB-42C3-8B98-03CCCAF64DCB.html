Business Strategies Workbench

The Business Strategies workbench is a tool for developing and maintaining lending product strategies through graphical and intuitive rule structures such as decision flows, rulesets, score models, scenarios, decision trees, and decision tables. You can also use the workbench to test these product strategies.

Covered Topics
  • Business Strategies Workbench User Interface
    The Business Strategies workbench has a intuitive user-friendly interface that allows you to create and maintain the components, such as rulesets, decision tables, decision flows, and so on.
  • Integration with Decision Modeler
    The Business Strategies workbench is integrated with FICO® Decision Modeler. You use the workbench to author business rules which are used for decisioning your credit applications. The Decision Modeler configures a decision service from the rules that you have authored and deploys it in the FICO® Decision Management Platform (DMP). A decision service automates a decision-making task. It is implemented as a set of business rules and exposed as a web service.
  • Analytic Data Mart
    Your decision service execution automatically logs event data to the Analytic Data Mart (ADM). The ADM captures and stores event data each time a client application invokes a decision service running on the Decision Management Platform (DMP).
  • Defining and Evaluating Business Strategies
    The Decision Module enables you to set up origination business strategies that suit your business practices.

    With the Decision Module, when naming objects, fields, or SMW components (such as rules, decision tables, and decision trees), use combinations of alphanumeric characters (A-Z, a-z, 0-9) and the underscore (_) only; do not use spaces.
