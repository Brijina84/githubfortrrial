Entering Balance Sheets for a Small Business Application

The Balance Sheets sub-pane appears under Business Financial Details in the Create Application page for small business applications.
To enter balance sheet information for a small business, perform the following steps:
  1. Enter the details in the fields provided under Current Assets, Non Current Assets, Current Liabilities, Long Term Liabilities, and Business Expenses.
  2. (Optional) Use the additional tabs on the Balance Sheets pane to enter the balance sheets for the previous two years.
  3. (Optional) Use Move to liabilities to move the Retained earnings field under Business Expenses. You can use Move to Assets to move the Retained earnings field back to Non Current Assets.
