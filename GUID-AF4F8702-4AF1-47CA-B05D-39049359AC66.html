Tasks

Tasks are activities in a business process or workflow. A task is the actual work being done in a workflow or business process.

The following types of tasks are available while defining a workflow or a sub-flow:

Invoke Workflow

The Invoke Workflow task calls another workflow or sub-flow from the current one. From the list of workflows that are already configured, you need to select the workflow that you want to invoke.
