Comparison Results for Categories and Products

When you compare two versions of a category, the differences in the properties of the category, and the names of its products and product selection trees are highlighted. When you compare two versions of a product, the differences in its properties and promotion values are highlighted.

Categories

In the Visual Differences window for categories, changes in category properties, and additions, modifications, deletions, and order changes to the Product selection tree and Product table rows are highlighted. The Jump to menu is available when there are changes to a product selection tree or product.
Note In the Product selection trees table, a row is highlighted as having been modified if there is a change in name. If the only changes are in the product selection tree's contents—for example, a changed variable mapping or a changed node property—that row is not highlighted. However, the name of the product selection tree is displayed as an option in the Jump to menu.

To view the differences in a product selection tree's contents, click its name in the Jump to menu. In the new Visual Differences window that appears, differences in the product selection tree's variables and nodes are displayed. To return to the window for category differences, click Category Properties in the Jump to menu.

Products

There two ways to view the differences between product versions:
  • As with product selection trees, by clicking the name of the product in the Jump to menu of the Visual Differences window for comparing category versions.
  • By selecting the two versions in the Version History table for the product, and clicking Compare.
