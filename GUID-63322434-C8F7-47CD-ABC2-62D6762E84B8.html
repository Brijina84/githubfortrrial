Activating or Deactivating Organizations

To activate or deactivate an organization, use the Activate or Deactivate button that appears near the top of the Organizations page. The button name changes depending on whether the organization is activated.
  1. In the administration navigation pane, click Organizations.
  2. (Optional) In the search area field, enter search filter criteria and click Search.
  3. Perform one of the following actions:
    • To deactivate an organization, select the check box next to the entry row and click Deactivate.
    • To activate an organization, click Show inactive Organizations or Show all, select the check box next to the entry row, and click Activate.
