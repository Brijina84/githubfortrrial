Left Navigation Pane

The Business Strategies drop-down menu shows Item Types, Test, and Tools. Each menu item has a left navigation pane whose options provide access to various aspects of the Business Strategies functionality. The functionality that you can access and use depends on your role and privileges.

Item Types

In the initial page of the Business Strategies workbench that appears when you log on, the Item Types tab is active, its left navigation page is displayed, and the list page for Categories and Products is shown on the right.

The left navigation pane of the Item Types tab lists all the item types available. While the Item Types tab is active, its left navigation pane is visible unless you choose to hide it. Click the Hide Left Panel arrow icon to hide the pane. To reinstate the pane, click the Show Left Panel arrow icon. To view the list page of an item type, click its name in the left navigation pane.

The left navigation pane of the Item Types menu item includes the following options:
  • Categories and Products—Create and manage categories and products
  • Product Strategies—Create and manage product strategies, which are specialized decision trees that determines which decision flow executes for a product.
  • Decision Flows—Create and manage the flow of control in a decision.
  • Data Object References—Create and maintain data object references to extract one or more objects that match specific conditions.
  • Data Methods—Create and manage procedures associated with a specific type of object.
  • Data Method Sequences—Create and manage ordered lists of data methods.
  • Rulesets—Create and manage categorical grouping of rules and the parameters associated with each included rule
  • Decision Tables—Define and maintain a large number of rules in a tabular format.
  • Decision Trees—Create and maintain rules in tree formats.
  • Score Models—Create and manage the formulae to used to predict the likely behavior of a customer or prospect.
  • Scenarios—Create and maintain specialized type of decision tree actions that represents possibleoutcomes.
  • Score Model Reasons—Create and maintain score model reason code lists to provide users with reasons as to why a particular score was assigned during evaluation.

Test

The Test tab allows you to test components and business strategies with different scenarios to ensure that the decisions returned suit your business practices.

The left navigation pane of the Test menu item includes the following options:
  • Test Execution—Add test cases and expected output for components and strategies. Run tests and view test results against expected output.
  • Base Applications—Create, import, and manage base applications.
  • Test Scenarios—Create and manage test data.

Tools

The Tools tab allows you to verify configured items, update your private workspace, and generate reports on configured and changed items that help in auditing your business strategies. The left navigation pane of the Tools tab includes the following options:
  • Verify All—Verify that all items configured in your business strategies are valid.
  • Strategy Component Snapshot—Generate a date-specific report of the items configured in your business strategies.
  • Update Workspace—Synchronize your private workspace with the contents of the configuration repository.
  • Object Model—View the Object Model schema and its version history.
  • Audit Strategies—Generate a report of item changes that were made within a specific period.
  • Publish— TBD
  • Excel Lookup Values—Upload, download, and delete the Excel files that are used in data methods. The Excel Lookup values help in decision making.
  • Custom Files and Functions—Upload, download, and delete custom files and functions. You can enable or disable custom functions. Custom function are used in data methods.
  • Manage Repository—Import and export the configuration repository.
Keep the following in mind while navigating within the user interface:
  • Use the options in the navigation panes, and not the browser Back and Forward (or Refresh) buttons. Using the browser buttons can cause you to lose any changes you have made to an item.
