Origination Manager Workbenches

Origination Manager workbenches help you configure and manage critical functionality, such as business strategies, application processing workflows, business object modeling, and

Business Strategies Workbench

Use the Business Strategies workbench to develop, test, and maintain lending product strategies through graphical and intuitive rule structures such as decision flows, rulesets, score models, scenarios, decision trees, and decision tables.

Business Processes Workbench

Use the Business Processes workbench to create and maintain workflows that you use to process credit applications; create and maintain the steps and logic to approve or decline an application.

Business Object Model Workbench

Use the Business Object Model workbench to define objects, fields, and relationships specific to your line of business.
