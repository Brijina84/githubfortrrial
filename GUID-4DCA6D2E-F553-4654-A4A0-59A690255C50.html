Decision Flows

A decision flow is a graphical representation of processing associated with a Decision Module request.
Decision flows are composed of steps that can include any of the following item types:
  • Data Method Sequence
  • Decision Table
  • Decision Tree
  • Ruleset
  • Score Model
After you have created the necessary decision flow components, you can incorporate them into a decision flow. You must assign one of the following components to a product:
  • A default decision flow
  • A product strategy containing at least one decision flow
