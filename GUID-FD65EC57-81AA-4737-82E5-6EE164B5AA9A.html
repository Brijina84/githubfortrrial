Deleting Score Ranges

Administrators with the proper permissions can delete the lower and upper limit score range values for a model category (line of business) used in the Final Score and Approval Rate Decomposition reports.

When you delete a score range, the changes immediately affect the Final Score and Approval Rate Decomposition reports because the reports are constructed from the values that exist at the time the report is run.

  1. In the administration navigation pane, click Reports Configuration > Score Range Configuration.
  2. Click the Delete icon in the row of the model category you want to remove.
