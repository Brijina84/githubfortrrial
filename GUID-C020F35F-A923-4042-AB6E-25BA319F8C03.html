Removing a Rule from a Ruleset

Use the Delete icon in the Rules section of the Ruleset Properties page to remove a rule from its associated ruleset.

The ruleset from which you want to remove the rule must be checked out and in edit mode.

  1. On the Ruleset Properties page, select the rule you want to remove.
  2. Click the Delete icon in the Rules section.
  3. When prompted to confirm the deletion, click Yes.
  4. Click Save.
