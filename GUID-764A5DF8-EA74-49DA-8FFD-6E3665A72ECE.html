Deleting Product Categories

Administrators with the proper permissions can delete product categories from the system. You cannot delete a product category that is associated with a product. If a product category is referenced within a product, you must first remove the product category from the product before deleting it.

When a product category is deleted, you can no longer create a product within that product category. For example, if you delete a product category, it will no longer appear in the Category drop-down lists on the Products > Create Product or Products > Edit <Product Name> pages.

If you delete a product category, you can create another product category with the same name as the deleted product category at a later time.

  1. In the administration navigation pane, click Categories.
  2. (Optional) In the search area field, enter search filter criteria and click Search.
  3. Select the product to delete and click the Delete Category icon for that row.
  4. Click OK in the confirmation dialog box that appears.
