Modifying Score Cutoffs

Use the Populations page to modify population descriptions and score cutoffs in the system.

  1. In the administration navigation pane, click Populations.
  2. (Optional) In the search area field, enter search filter criteria and click Search.
  3. Select the population to modify and click the Edit Population icon for that row.
  4. Make the desired modifications to the description, score cutoff, model category, or status.
  5. Click Save.
