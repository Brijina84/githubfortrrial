Viewing Business Details

The Business Details pane on the Application <Number> page for a small business credit application contains the following current details about the business. This pane appears only for small business credit applications.
  • Business Bureau Report (if data is available)
    Note The following Business Bureau Report is provided as an example.
  • Balance Sheets
    • Assets (Current and Non Current Assets)
    • Liabilities (Current and Long Term Liabilities, and Business Expenses)
  • Income Statements
    • Income (from sales and other sources)
    • Expenses (from sales and other reasons)
  • Existing Credit Obligations
    • Product Type
    • Payment
    • Payment Type
    • Rate Spread
    • Rate Type
    • Amount Authorized
    • Outstanding Balance
    • Current Exposure
    • Potential Exposure
