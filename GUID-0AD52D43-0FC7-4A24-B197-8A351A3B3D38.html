Activating and Deactivating Populations

To activate or deactivate a population, use the Activate or Deactivate button that appears near the top of the Populations page. The button name changes depending on whether the population is activated.
Note The status of a new score cutoff defaults to Inactive. You must change it to Active because inactive populations cannot be used in scoring.
  1. In the administration navigation pane, click Populations.
  2. (Optional) In the search area field, enter search filter criteria and click Search.
  3. Perform one of the following actions:
    • To deactivate a population, select the check box next to the entry row and click Deactivate.
    • To activate a population, click Show inactive populations or Show all, select the check box next to the row, and click Activate.
